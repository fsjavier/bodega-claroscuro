Discover our Wine seleciton

At Bodega Claroscuro, we have a twofold commitment: to create wines of the highest quality and to share our passion with others. Through each bottle, we aim to capture the unique terroir of our vineyards, allowing the flavors and aromas to tell a story that reflects our dedication to craftsmanship.
We are thrilled to introduce you to our youngest wines and keep you updated on our vineyard and wines.

Discover "Sabor del Norte", our latest creation from the renown Tempranillo grape. Crafted with the greatest passion and dedication, embodies our traditional values. With a deep ruby color and complex aroma of cherry and spices pairs beautifully with a range of culinary delights. From this summer in our shop!
